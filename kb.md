# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Product Overview
AutoStream is a SaaS product that provides automated video editing tools for content creators.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price**: $29/month
- **Features**:
    - 10 videos/month
    - 720p resolution

### Pro Plan
- **Price**: $79/month
- **Features**:
    - Unlimited videos
    - 4K resolution
    - AI captions
    - 24/7 support

## Company Policies
- **Refunds**: No refunds after 7 days of purchase.
- **Support**: 24/7 support is available ONLY on the Pro plan. Basic plan users can access email support during business hours.
